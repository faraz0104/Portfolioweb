How Much Does a Website Cost in India? (2025 Complete Guide)

Planning to build a website in India but confused about pricing? This guide breaks down real costs for every type of website — from a simple landing page to a full-scale e-commerce store.

⏱ 6 min read

One of the most common questions I get from business owners in India is: "How much will my website cost?" The honest answer is — it depends. But in this guide, I'll give you real, transparent numbers so you can budget properly.

Factors That Affect Website Cost in India

Before we get to numbers, here's what influences the final price:

  • Type of website — landing page, business site, blog, or e-commerce?
  • Design — template-based vs. fully custom design
  • Features — contact forms, booking systems, payment gateways, login portals
  • Content management — do you need a CMS like WordPress to edit it yourself?
  • Developer location — freelancer vs. agency, India vs. abroad
  • SEO setup — basic meta tags or full technical SEO implementation

What's Included in the Price?

A good web developer in India should include all of the following in their quote:

Design & Development

  • Custom or template-based responsive design
  • Mobile & tablet optimization
  • Cross-browser compatibility (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Contact forms and basic interactivity

SEO Foundation (Very Important)

  • Proper meta titles and descriptions for each page
  • Semantic HTML structure (H1, H2, H3 hierarchy)
  • Image alt tags and compressed images
  • XML sitemap and robots.txt
  • Google Analytics integration

Hosting & Domain (Usually Extra)

  • Domain name: ₹700 – ₹1,500/year (.com or .in)
  • Shared hosting: ₹2,500 – ₹6,000/year (Hostinger, SiteGround)
  • VPS/Cloud hosting: ₹5,000 – ₹20,000/year
  • Vercel/Netlify (static sites): Free for basic use

Cheap vs. Quality — What's the Real Cost?

Many businesses in India opt for the cheapest option — a ₹5,000 "website" from a local shop or a DIY Wix site. Here's what you often get:

  • Slow loading speed (hurts SEO and user experience)
  • No proper SEO setup (Google won't rank it)
  • Generic templates that look identical to hundreds of other sites
  • No mobile optimization
  • Zero support after delivery

A website is your digital salesperson — it works 24/7. Investing ₹40,000–₹80,000 in a proper website that ranks on Google and converts visitors will pay for itself many times over.

"Your website is not a cost — it's an investment. A well-built website in India can generate leads worth lakhs every year."

Hidden Costs to Watch Out For

  • Revisions after delivery — ask how many are included upfront
  • Content writing — some developers charge extra for writing page content
  • Image licenses — stock photos can add ₹3,000–₹15,000
  • Payment gateway integration — Razorpay/Paytm setup may cost extra
  • Annual maintenance — budget ₹5,000–₹15,000/year for updates and security

The Bottom Line

For a professional, SEO-optimised business website in India in 2025, budget ₹40,000 – ₹80,000. For e-commerce, plan for ₹80,000 – ₹2,00,000. Don't go below ₹20,000 unless it's a very simple landing page — quality has a price.
