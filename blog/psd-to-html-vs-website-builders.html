PSD to HTML vs Website Builders: Which is Right for You?

Should you use Wix, Squarespace, or a custom-coded website? This honest comparison covers performance, SEO, cost, and flexibility — so you can make the right choice.

F
Faraz Khan
Web Developer & SEO Expert
📅 June 1, 2025
⏱ 5 min read

You've got a design — maybe a beautiful Figma mockup or a PSD file from a designer. Now what? Do you convert it to clean HTML/CSS, or just drag it into Wix or Squarespace? This is one of the most common decisions businesses face in India today.

What is PSD to HTML Conversion?

PSD to HTML (also called Figma to HTML or design to code) is the process of taking a visual design file — created in Photoshop (PSD), Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch — and converting it into live, hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

The result is a pixel-perfect, clean-coded website that matches your design exactly, loads fast, and can be integrated with any backend or CMS.

What Are Website Builders?

Website builders like Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, and WordPress.com are drag-and-drop platforms that let you build a website without writing code. You pick a template, customise it visually, and publish.

They're popular because they're fast and require no technical knowledge. But they come with significant trade-offs.

The SEO Difference is Huge

This is where custom HTML/CSS wins decisively. Website builders add bloated code — unnecessary JavaScript, inline styles, and extra HTTP requests — that slow down your site significantly.

Google's Core Web Vitals measure page speed and user experience as ranking factors. A custom-coded site consistently scores 90–100 on PageSpeed Insights. Most Wix or Squarespace sites score 50–70.

A 1-second delay in page load time reduces conversions by 7%. Page speed is both a UX and SEO issue.

When to Use a Website Builder

Website builders are appropriate when:

  • You need a very simple personal portfolio or hobby site
  • You have zero budget and need something live quickly
  • You want to manage all content yourself with no technical help
  • It's a temporary landing page for an event

When to Use PSD to HTML / Custom Development

Choose custom development when:

  • You have a specific design that must be implemented exactly
  • SEO and page speed are important to your business
  • You want a professional, unique website that stands out
  • You're building an e-commerce store or web application
  • You want full ownership and portability of your code
  • Your business depends on online visibility and lead generation

What About WordPress?

WordPress (self-hosted, not WordPress.com) is a great middle ground — it's a CMS (Content Management System) that lets you manage content easily, while a developer can build a fully custom theme on top of it.

This gives you the best of both worlds: custom, fast, SEO-friendly code with an easy-to-use dashboard for updating content. At DevNova, we specialise in converting PSDs to pixel-perfect WordPress themes.

Which Should You Choose?

If your business depends on online visibility — if you want clients to find you on Google — invest in a custom PSD to HTML or WordPress website. The upfront cost is higher, but the long-term ROI from better SEO, higher trust, and faster performance far outweighs the savings from a free website builder.
